The PS1 Classic is on the way!

A few months back, Sony revealed the PS1 Classic that will include 20 games and 2 original PS1 controllers. Releasing on December 3rd, the PS1 Classic will be similar to Nintendos successful NES and SNES Classics with save states and an HD graphic upgrade.

The games revealed are listed:

  1. Final Fantasy VII
  2. Tekken 3
  3. Battle Arena Toshinden
  4. Rayman
  5. Metal Gear Solid
  6. Wild Arms
  7. Jumping Flash
  8. R4: Ridge Racer Type 4
  9. Mr.Driller
  10. Super Puzzle Fighter II Turbo
  11. Syphon Filter
  12. Resident Evil Directors Cut
  13. Intelligent Qube
  14. Cool Boarders 2
  15. Twisted Metal
  16. Grand Theft Auto
  17. Destruction Derby
  18. Oddworld: Abe's Odyssee
  19. Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six
  20. Revelations: Persona

Red Dead Redemption II Online Update

Rockstar Games and parent company Take-Two Interactive have confirmed that RDR2 Multiplayer is being launched near the end of November. With November ending, it looks like we should be getting some kind of announcement about RDR2 very shortly.
